Who I am

I'm a cloud engineer and AI automation specialist based in Nairobi, Kenya, and co-founder of TriqFlow Results, where I lead infrastructure and deployment. My path here started in the Nairobi AI community — an n8n training course that turned into the foundation TriqFlow was built on.

Day-to-day, I work across AWS, n8n, and Google Cloud — designing automations, self-hosting workflow infrastructure, and building the kind of tooling that catches problems before they become expensive. LambdaCostWatchdog, one of my recent projects, came directly out of a stray Elastic IP that quietly racked up AWS charges — a good reminder that infrastructure work is as much about watching systems as building them.

I'm currently working toward AWS Solutions Architect Associate certification and pursuing the Anthropic Claude Partner Network pathway for TriqFlow.
